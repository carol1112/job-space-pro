Frequently Asked Questions

What is Job Space Pro?

+

Job Space Pro is a remote staffing agency connecting businesses with pre-vetted global professionals. We specialize in flexible hiring solutions (full-time, part-time, freelance) across industries like tech, marketing, design, and customer support.

How does Job Space Pro find remote talent?

+

We use a rigorous vetting process combining AI-powered screening and human interviews to identify top-tier remote professionals with proven track records in distributed work environments.

What industries do you specialize in?

+

We recruit for various digital-first industries including tech, customer support, digital marketing, software development, and virtual project management.

How long does the hiring process take?

+

Our streamlined process typically matches businesses with qualified candidates within 7-10 business days, depending on role specificity.
